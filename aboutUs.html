About us

JCC is a famous conference center located in Jakarta, Indonesia. JCC is one of the earliest as well as popular convention centers in Jakarta. First opened in 1974, JCC many important national and international conference, exhibition, fair, indoor sports, and musical concerts were held at JCC including 11th Non-Aligned Movement Conference and Asian-African Conference. It is a very venue place throughout the year. List of certificates: ISO 9001, ISO 14001, OHSAS 18001OHSAS 18001, ISO 45001, and many more.
